 * --primary & --primary-foreground: The main   
      interactive color (like links) and the      
     text color that goes on top of it.
   * --secondary & --secondary-foreground: Used   
      for less prominent elements that still      
     need to stand out.
   * --accent & --accent-foreground: For
     elements that need to draw attention.        
   * --muted & --muted-foreground: For subtle     
     text and backgrounds.
   * --background & --foreground: The main        
     background and text colors for the entire    
     site.
   * --card & --card-foreground: The background   
      and text colors for card-like containers.   
   * --border: The color used for borders.
